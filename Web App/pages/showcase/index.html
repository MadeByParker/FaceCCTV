Start Solving Crimes.

FaceCCTV is a facial recognition app that allows users to upload images of people they want to identify. The app then uses deep learning facial detection technology to identify the person in the image, alongside image enhancement technology to improve the quality.

Hands Free.

Just upload an image and let the AI do the rest. FaceCCTV will automatically detect the face(s) in the image and enhance the image.

Deployment

FaceCCTV provides cross platform functionality using numerous technologies, React Native and Electron. The app is available on both iOS and Android. The app is also available on the web and desktop.

User Friendly Interface and Experience

The app was built with a user friendly interface and experience in mind by using Tailwind's modern styling framework. The app is easy to use and navigate. Along with customisable settings and options are available to the user.
